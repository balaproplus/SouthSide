From the streets of Tamil Nadu to the heart of India from every artist’s own mind and efficiency, South Side Hip Hop Records is the home for raw, independent rap talent that refuses to be silenced.Welcome to South Side Hip Hop Records India Born in the heart of Chennai, Tamil Nadu — South Side Hip Hop Records India is a home for hip-hop artists, rappers, and storytellers across India. It’s The Foundation of Real Sound, South Side Hip Hop Records India was born out of need, a need for a space where real artists can breathe, create, and be heard. No gimmicks. No mumble. No fake flex. Just authentic music that represents struggle, pride, roots, rebellion, and the fight to rise. Whether you’re a kid writing bars in your notebook, a survivor pouring your pain onto a beat, or an underground MC ready to take over stages , South Side Hip Hop Records India is your home.

Our Movement

we carry that spirit forward - raw, unfiltered, and unbreakable. We exist to create space for the dreamers, the fighters, the ones who turn their life stories into anthems.

We are building not just songs - we are building a culture, a community, a legacy that will echo for generations. From underground cyphers to main stages, from healing personal scars to shaking entire crowds.

The Foundation And Vision

We exists to educate, empower, and elevate. Laying the Groundwork for a Movement. It is the soul beneath the sound. It’s not just about dropping tracks — it’s about dropping truth. Our mission is to create real structure in the Indian music industry for hip-hop and rap.

Nurturing lyrical skill and Educating upcoming artists about hip-hop's roots and their own histories.

Building creative independence that leads to long-term careers.

Supporting voices that deserve to be heard without compromise.

Building together a path to Freedom.
